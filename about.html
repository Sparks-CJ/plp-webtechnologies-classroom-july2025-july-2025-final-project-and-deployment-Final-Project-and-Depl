About WakulimaHub

WakulimaHub is a peer-to-peer agriculture platform that connects farmers, buyers, and advisors. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers by giving them access to markets and farming knowledge in one place.

Our Vision

To build sustainable farming communities where knowledge and trade flow freely, improving livelihoods and food security.

What We Offer

  • A digital marketplace for buying and selling farm produce
  • Access to farming advisory and shared knowledge
  • A trusted network of local farmers and buyers
