About WakulimaHub

Empowering farmers with access to markets, knowledge, and support for sustainable agriculture.

Our Story

WakulimaHub was founded with a simple mission: to connect smallholder farmers with buyers, agricultural experts, and resources that help them thrive. We believe that agriculture is not just about farming — it’s about building communities, ensuring food security, and creating opportunities.

What We Stand For

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly farming practices for long-term growth and resilience.

Community

Connecting farmers, buyers, and experts to build stronger agricultural networks.

Innovation

Using technology to simplify access to markets and knowledge for every farmer.
